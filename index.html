About STK Tailorings

Welcome to STK Tailorings, a women-run, women-focused tailoring service rooted in traditional values and modern excellence. Under the guidance of S. Tamilselvi, we bring together the timeless art of tailoring with contemporary fitting techniques.

Our workspace is a sanctuary where women can freely discuss their needs, share their visions, and receive personalized attention. We believe in the spiritual connection between craft and devotion, honoring Lord Shiva's blessings in every stitch we create.

Located in the heart of Pennadam, we serve the community with integrity, quality, and dedication. Every garment tells a story, and we are honored to be part of yours.
